About Dr. Naresh Thakur

Dr. Naresh Thakur is a seasoned clinical psychologist with over 12 years of experience, dedicated to helping you navigate life’s complexities with clarity and confidence. Specializing in anxiety, stress, and personal growth, he creates a nurturing space for transformation.

With a Ph.D. in Psychology from the University of Mumbai and expertise in cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT) and mindfulness, Dr. Thakur blends science with empathy. Clients praise his warm, insightful approach that feels like a conversation with a trusted guide.

Passionate about mental wellness, Dr. Thakur enjoys meditation, community outreach, and sharing insights to destigmatize therapy.
